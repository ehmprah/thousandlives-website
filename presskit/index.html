Team

Thousand Lives is the first game of emfinite studio. The german-french-canadian indie studio is comprised of Mario Kaiser, Mathias Tournier and Michael Champion and hails from Berlin and Vancouver.
Thousand Lives was first conceived years ago by Mario "ehmprah" Kaiser, who until now was busy making Coregrounds and its spin-off Core Defense. But success of the latter laid the foundation to finally make this passion project a reality.
